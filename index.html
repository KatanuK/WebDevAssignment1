THE LITTLE BAKERY

"Joy in every bite"




The Little Bakery is online-based and delivers a variety of freshly baked goods to your doorstep. We strive to produce the best quality for our customers at affordable prices ensuring that we spread joy, satisfaction and happiness through our products.We provide a large range of products catering for the tastes of all people; young and old, big and small, One bite into any of our delicious baked goodies brings a smile to anyone's face.

  • - Cookies
  • - Muffins
  • - Brownies
  • - Croissants


For any further inquiries about our business please feel free to contact us through call or email and we will provide an answer as promptly as we can.



ABOUT THE OWNER

Kiara Greenwood is the founder and current owner of the little bakery.
She was born and raised in the Peterborough, UK but in her 20s travelled to Kenya where she found her forever home as she instantly fell in love with the country and it's people.
After working as a pharmacist for 20 years she finally found her true calling: Baking. Thus The Little Bakery was born and continues to grow every day.
